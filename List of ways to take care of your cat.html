Tips for Cat Care

  • Make sure that your cat gets plenty of sleep as cats like to sleep 14 to 18 hours per day
    • Cats like to sleep in these places:
      • The Floor
      • Cat Tree
      • Cat Bed
      • Your Bed
  • Here is how to clean their litter box:
    1. Scoop out potty box.
    2. Remove contents.
    3. Add new litter if needed.
  • Cats are picky eaters, but here is some foods that they like:
    • Fish
    • Chicken
    • Beef
    • Turkey
